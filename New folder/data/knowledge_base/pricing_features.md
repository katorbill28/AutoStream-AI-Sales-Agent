# AutoStream Pricing and Features

AutoStream is an AI-driven lead engagement platform for inbound social and website conversations. It qualifies prospects, answers product questions, and routes sales-ready leads to the right team.

## Plans

Starter plan: $99 per month for up to 3 team members, 2,000 conversations per month, email support, and a shared reporting dashboard.

Growth plan: $249 per month for up to 10 team members, 10,000 conversations per month, CRM integrations, custom lead-routing rules, and priority support.

Enterprise plan: Custom pricing for unlimited seats, SSO, dedicated onboarding, advanced analytics, and SLA-backed support.

## Key Features

AutoStream supports omnichannel lead intake, FAQ automation, qualification flows, CRM enrichment, and handoff to human sales representatives.

Integrations include HubSpot, Salesforce, Slack, Zapier, and webhook-based custom workflows.

The analytics dashboard tracks conversations, lead conversion, qualification rate, and response times.

Onboarding includes a knowledge-base import, playbook setup, and launch support for paid plans.
