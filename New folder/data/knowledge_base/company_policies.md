# AutoStream Company Policies

## Security and Data Handling

AutoStream encrypts data in transit and at rest. Enterprise customers can request audit logs and SSO configuration support.

Customer conversation data is retained for 12 months by default. Enterprise retention policies can be customized during onboarding.

## Support Policy

Starter customers receive email support during business hours. Growth customers receive priority email support. Enterprise customers receive a dedicated success contact and SLA-backed response targets.

## Billing and Cancellation

Plans are billed monthly. Customers can upgrade at any time, and changes take effect immediately for the new billing cycle.

Starter and Growth plans can be canceled before the next renewal date. Enterprise contracts follow the signed order form and agreed billing terms.

Refunds are not issued for partial billing periods, but plan downgrades are applied at the next renewal.
